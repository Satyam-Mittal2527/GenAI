The Spirit of Cricket

On a field of green beneath the sky,
A ball takes flight, a dream rides high.
The bat sings loud with every stroke,
While cheers arise like clouds of smoke.

A bowler runs with steady pace,
Determination on every face.
The wicket waits, the crowd holds breath,
Each over tells a tale of depth.

Victory smiles on those who strive,
Who keep the spirit of the game alive.
Win or lose, stand proud and true,
For cricket brings out the best in you.

So lift your bat, let courage shine,
Enjoy each moment, every line.
For more than runs or trophies bright,
Cricket is passion, heart, and light.
